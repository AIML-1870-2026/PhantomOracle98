About Scamazon

This Product Review Generator is a dynamic web application that uses Large Language Models (LLMs) to generate product reviews based on your inputs.

You can customize the product name, category, review length, writing style, sentiment, and more. The app supports both Anthropic (Claude) and OpenAI models.

Note: AI-generated reviews are for educational and research purposes only. Publishing AI-generated reviews as genuine consumer reviews may violate FTC regulations effective October 21, 2024.
